About Me

Written by Emily Griffen

I grew up in the Hudson Valley in upstate New York. I went to Skidmore College in Saratoga Springs, where I studied art with a concentration in Communication Design. I moved to Atlanta in 2016 and work for Pottery Barn.

Here are some interesting things about me:

  • I'm inspired by my grandfather who was an art director for Time Magazine in the Madmen era.
  • I have a brother who's a year and a half older than me and we are currently living together.
  • I studied art in Greece and Italy for a semester with a school called the Aegean Center.
  • My middle name is Carles after my great, great uncle Arthur B. Carles who was a painter.
